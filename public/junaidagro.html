JUNAID® AGRO

Your agro solution provider

Our desire to meet your changing needs and requirements come first, through our innovative approaches in delivering value to you.

Since its registration in 2013, JAP Limited provides quality assurance in order to ensure that our JUNAID® Frites product is safe for human consumption. In order to deliver a healthy final product, standard manufacturing guidelines are complied with and observed to ensure consistent, acceptable product quality, and this is made possible through our trained & qualified Personnel, facilities and equipment in good condition, reliable processes and procedures, safe products for consumer usage.

Because continual improvement is our focus, we engage in quality checks at different production levels, through the use of modern technologies, innovative approaches amongst others. Good Manufacturing Practices are recommended with the goal of safeguarding the health of consumers as well as producing good quality Fries.

Junaid AGRO

JUNAID Frites (Frozen French Fries) is the flagship product of JUNAID Agro Product (JAP) Limited. From inception, we seek to build and enhance local capacity in growing our raw materials and aim to create a world class product and brand.

OUR MISION

To create food products from local agro produce in order to make them available & accessible far and wide.

OUR VISION

To become one of the leading food companies in Africa by contributing in growing the agricultural value chain in Nigeria.
