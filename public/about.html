ABOUT US

Company Background & History

Junaid is an Integrated Services Company with specialization in two major sectors; Energy and Agriculture. Its Head Office is located in the economic nerve of the Country, Lagos, Nigeria. Our company operates a dynamic business structure manned and managed by a team of qualified and seasoned professionals who bring into the company, several years of project experience in their respective fields of specialization. For us, the conviction of positive symbiotic combination of expertise has brought this team of professionals together with a view to forming a formidable technically and professionally competent Nigerian organization.

Prior to incorporation of JUNAID Synergy Limited, and since 2000, working through our other companies; JUNAID Integrated Services Limited, Silikon Ink Limited, NIMAN GROUP, we have continued to strive towards establishing a mark in our business endeavors to ensure that we live up to our corporate objective and exceptional standard.

Like other knowledge-age companies, JUNAID boasts of Project Management, Resource Sourcing, Public Works, Consulting, Resource Management, amidst others.

Our ambition and goal, is “To strive tenaciously towards building a sustainable environment, with the ability to create cutting-edge solutions that would invariably result to productivity through our exceptional and quality service”.
